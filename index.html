// What we do

Advancing public welfare
with the Lightning Network, Blockchain Technologies & Layer 2 Solutions

Digital Playhouse Foundation is an Australian registered charity, public benevolent institution and deductible gift recipient - returning surplus to its programs and communities.

Our projects are advancing digital and financial literacy, and adapting existing skills to emerging technology to assist individual adults and kids, and aligned social enterprises growing the social and monetary capital in their communities - relieving disadvantage now and into the future.

// Current projects

What we're building

01 / PROJECT

CREDU

Accountants On-Chain - bringing the accounting profession into the Lightning Network and Blockchain Technologies era through structured education and credentialing.

BitByBit + CREDU
02 / PROJECT

Stacks Australia

Building the Australian node of the Stacks ecosystem - enabling smart contracts secured by cryptographic proof-of-work for Australian developers and communities. Supported by the DPF Learner Fund, this project creates pathways for Australians to participate in next-generation decentralised application development.

STX AUS
03 / SECURITY

Opsec Academy

Empowering businesses and educational institutions with comprehensive operational security training. Protect your organisation from evolving cyber threats.

Professional OpSec Workshops
04 / NOSTR

Digital Portfolio

A Nostr-native portfolio platform for learners to own, control, and showcase their educational journey. Built on decentralised relays - learners own their content, forever.

Built on Nostr ⚡
05 / EDUCATION

Network School

Partnering with Network School to support individuals most at risk of being left behind by the digital economy. Through structured upskilling programs in emerging technologies, Blockchain Technologies, and Layer 2 Solutions, we help people build the skills and confidence to participate in the future of work.

Upskilling & Digital Inclusion
06 / COMMUNITY

SPARK Centre - Boneo Primary

DPF has donated hardware and computers to establish the SPARK Centre at Boneo Primary School - enabling next-level digital education from Prep through to Year 6. The centre equips young learners with hands-on access to the technologies shaping their future, right from the start of their schooling journey.

Hardware Donation · P - 6
07 / RESEARCH

Decentralised Learning Protocol

A formal whitepaper outlining an open-source, community-governed education protocol built on the Lightning Network - self-sovereign digital identity via Nostr, project-based learning, zero debt-based tuition. Published under GNU GPL v3.0.

Whitepaper · Open Source
08 / EDUCATION

Value 4 Value Lessons

Lightning Network-native learning experiences running since 2018 - structured lessons built around the Value 4 Value model, rewarding participation with real sats. Now building a dedicated platform to host and scale these lessons globally.

V4V · Lightning Network Education
